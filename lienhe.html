Frequently Asked Questions

How to place an order online?

You can place an order online by: 1) Selecting a product from the catalog, 2) Adding it to the cart, 3) Filling in the shipping information, 4) Choosing the payment method and confirming the order.

How long does the delivery take?

Delivery time is 1-3 working days within Ho Chi Minh City, 3-5 days for neighboring provinces, and 5-7 days for remote provinces.

Is there a return policy?

Yes, we have a return policy within 30 days from the date of purchase, provided that the product is intact and all accessories are included.

What is the product warranty policy?

All products come with a manufacturer's warranty ranging from 12 to 24 months depending on the type of product. You can bring the product to the warranty center or contact the hotline for assistance.

Is there a payment installment option?

Yes, we offer 0% interest installment payment through credit cards from partner banks. You can choose to pay in installments of 3, 6, 12, or 24 months.
